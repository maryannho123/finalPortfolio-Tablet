About Me

Hi, my name is Mary. I grew up with my four siblings and a very loud pet bird in Boulder Colorado. From growing up right next to the flatirons, I am an avid hiker and skiier. My childhoods consisted on camping in the Rockies and family bike rides along the Colorado River.

I am currently a Junior majoring in Computer Science through the College of Enginnering. I have experience in machine learning, computer vision, data processing, and research. I am actively engaged in the Society of Women of Engineers as I am one of the girl scout outreach officers. One of my passions is increasing accesibbility of STEM opportunities to young girls.

Outside of school, I am an avid watcher of Bachelor Nation. I also love baking and traveling the world with my family. Music is a big part of my life as I actively played the violin for 15 years
